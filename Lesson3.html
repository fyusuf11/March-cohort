Three step pancake recipe

Get ready to make some yummy pancakes!

    INGREDIENTS:
  • Oats
  • Banana
  • Almond milk
    Steps:
  1. Blend oats
  2. Add banana & almond milk to blended oats
  3. Make sure its not too runny
  4. Pour your mix on to a pan. And enjoy!
